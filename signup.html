We are but slaves to the whims of our higher ups. But one thing about me is, that I can spin. Oh I can spin. It'd be hard not to spin on a webpage such as this. So do me a favor and sit back while I spin some magic to get that word count up. And get ready to be convinved


Why sign up?? Well, I'm so glad you asked. Because you might as well acknowledge that we already know you're here. Gee, the whole internet really knows you're here. Take back the handles on this thing you call browsing man! And yeah, it might be a placebo effect. But don't you know those lowkey work?! Alright. Now get to it soldier. Make me proud.
